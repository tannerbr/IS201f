The Kenya Nairobi Mission 2018-2020

The Field is White Already to Harvest

My mission did not start easy. I had many days where all I could do was to think about how long two years was. I thought "if these last few days have felt long, how long will two years feel?" But luckily things got better faster than I thought.

I was able to go on and meet and teach many amazing and kind people. People of spirits of faith and optimisim.

Looking Back

As I get further and further away from that experience, I am still reminded of the wonderful experience it was and how much I was taught about the world. I came to know mysefl and God better in a way only the struggles and people of Kenya could teach me.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what mission is all about.

I met so many wonderful people during my time in Kenya

Those experiences were some of the best in my life. I use the lessons I learned in Africa everyday, I will be forever grateful for those amazing two years.
